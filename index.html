Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

You can book an appointment through our website by clicking the "Book Appointment" button, selecting your preferred department and doctor, choosing a date and time slot, and filling in your details. You'll receive instant confirmation via SMS and email.

Telemedicine allows you to consult with doctors via video call from the comfort of your home. Simply book a video consultation, join the call at your scheduled time, and receive a digital prescription after the consultation. All calls are secure and HIPAA-compliant.

Yes, we accept all major health insurance plans including Star Health, Apollo Munich, Max Bupa, ICICI Lombard, HDFC Ergo, and Bajaj Allianz. We also offer cashless treatment for approved insurance holders. Contact our insurance desk for verification.

Our outpatient department (OPD) is open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7. Sunday OPD is available from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM for select departments. Telemedicine consultations are available round the clock.

You can access all your medical records through our Patient Portal. Log in with your registered mobile number to view appointment history, prescriptions, lab reports, and medical records. You can also download reports and share them with other doctors.

Please bring a valid ID proof, your insurance card (if applicable), any previous medical records or prescriptions, and a list of current medications. If you have booked online, bring your booking confirmation. Arrive 15 minutes before your appointment time.
